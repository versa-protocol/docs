# Custodial Mode

By default, receipts are passed from senders to receivers without passing through Versa's servers:

![A diagram outlining the steps involved in sending a receipt from sender to receiver.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/versa-protocol/docs/main/assets/versa_diagram.svg)

However, in development, you may choose to initially have Versa handle the encryption and decryption.
